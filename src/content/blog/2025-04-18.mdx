# Pneumothorax
## Signs & Symptoms
“PneumoTHORAX”
- *P* Pleuritic pain
– *T* Tracheal deviation
– *H* Hyperresonance (and hemodynamic instability)
– *O* Onset sudden
– *R* Reduced breath sounds (and dyspnea)
– *A* Absent fremitus
– *X* X-ray show collapse.
